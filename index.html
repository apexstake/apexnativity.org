Hundreds of nativity exhibits from around the world set against beautiful, live music performances

Join us in Apex, North Carolina for this year's 11th annual celebration of the birth and ministry of our Lord Jesus Christ. This wonderful, three-day, annual celebration provides an opportunity for each of us to reflect on the true meaning of Christmas and find spiritual rest during the busy holiday season.
