Hundreds of nativity exhibits from around the world set against beautiful, live music performances

Join us in Apex, North Carolina for this year's 13th annual celebration of the birth and ministry of our Lord Jesus Christ. This wonderful, three-day, annual celebration provides an opportunity for each of us to reflect on the true meaning of Christmas and find spiritual rest during the busy holiday season.

The Apex Nativity Celebration is open for all members of the community to both visit and participate in. It is a free public event sponsored by the Apex Stake of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.

Community members are invited to loan nativity scenes for exhibit, enjoy the many musical performances by groups from the community, and join in the Messiah sing-in on Saturday evening.

A World of Nativities

Exhibitors throughout the region, who represent many faiths, loan their treasured nativities for the celebration. Each nativity offers a unique representation of the birth of Christ. Preview a sample of the variety of nativites you can expect to enjoy at this year's celebration.
